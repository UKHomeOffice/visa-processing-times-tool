## More information

Processing times start from either:

- the day of your appointment, if you proved your identity in person
- the working day after you finished uploading your documents, if you used the [‘UK Immigration: ID Check’ app](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/using-the-uk-immigration-id-check-app)

Processing times are based on the UK working week (Monday to Friday) and include public holidays in the UK. They do not include public holidays in other countries.
